About Quality Industrial Solution

Your industrial supply partner in Ajman, UAE

Quality Industrial Solution specializes in sourcing and supplying industrial machinery parts and equipment—from electrical and automation components to hydraulic systems, bearings, and engineering vehicle parts. We focus on reliability, brand quality, and clear communication on every inquiry.

What we do
We support factories, workshops, and industrial users with:
  • Electrical components and circuit protection
  • Power & distribution equipment
  • Automation & control systems
  • Pneumatic & hydraulic solutions
  • Bearings and engineering vehicle parts
How we work
Every inquiry is handled with attention to detail:
  • Clear, honest communication—no overpromising
  • Exact matches where possible, suitable alternatives when needed
  • Focus on availability, lead time, and brand reliability
Where we serve
Based in Ajman, UAE, we serve:
  • Local industries in Ajman, Sharjah, Dubai, and across the UAE
  • Workshops, factories, and maintenance teams
  • Clients needing fast, accurate sourcing of industrial parts
