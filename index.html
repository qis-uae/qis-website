About Quality Industrial Solution

Quality Industrial Solution is dedicated to supplying reliable industrial components and machinery parts, backed by global brands and a commitment to accurate, application‑focused sourcing.

Who we are

Quality Industrial Solution operates as a focused industrial supply partner, connecting end‑users, maintenance teams, and project contractors with the right components for their applications. The portfolio spans electrical components, power and distribution equipment, automation and control systems, encoders and DRO solutions, pneumatic and hydraulic products, bearings, printing industry components, and engineering vehicle parts.

Product Portfolio

The product range covers electrical components, power and distribution, automation and control, motion and feedback systems, pneumatic and hydraulic solutions, combustion control, bearings, printing components, engineering vehicle parts, and supporting consumables.

Electrical Components

Core electrical components for industrial panels, control systems, and power distribution assemblies.

  • Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB), MCCB, ACB, RCCB, fuse units
  • Contactors, overload relays, motor starters, soft starters
  • Relays, timers, terminal blocks, power supplies, protection modules
Brands: Siemens, Schneider Electric, ABB, Allen‑Bradley, Honeywell, Phoenix Contact, Omron, Mitsubishi Electric

Power & Distribution Equipment

Components for safe and efficient power distribution in industrial and commercial environments.

  • Switchgear and distribution boards
  • Panel components and busbars
  • Circuit protection and auxiliary equipment
Designed for robust, long‑term operation in demanding installations.

Automation & Control Systems

Integrated automation solutions for process, machine, and motion control.

  • PLC systems, remote I/O, industrial PCs, communication modules
  • Variable frequency drives (VFD), servo drives, servo motors
  • HMI panels, touch screens, industrial monitors
  • CNC controllers, positioning systems, signal converters, interface modules
Supports scalable automation architectures and modern control strategies.

Encoders, DRO & Coupling Systems

Precision feedback and measurement solutions for motion control and positioning.

  • Digital readouts (DRO), linear and rotary encoders
  • Angle encoders, position sensors, feedback modules
  • Counters, interpolators, encoder couplings
Suitable for CNC machines, automation lines, and precision equipment.

Pneumatic & Hydraulic Solutions

Actuation, control, and fluid power components for industrial automation and machinery.

  • Pneumatic cylinders, grippers, rotary actuators, air motors
  • Solenoid valves, pressure regulation, flow and throttle valves
  • FRL units, electro‑pneumatic proportional valves
  • Hydraulic pumps, cylinders, directional valves, pressure and flow control valves
  • Hoses, fittings, hydraulic accessories, suction and vacuum cups
Brands: SMC, CKD, AirTAC, FESTO, Norgren, Aventics, Parker, Rexroth, and others.

Combustion Control & Gas Equipment

Components for safe and precise combustion and gas handling systems.

  • Ratio regulators, zero governors, pressure regulators
  • Pressure switches for gas and air
  • Multifunctional gas controls, slam‑shut valves
  • Automatic shutoff and vent valves, EPV valves
  • Two‑stage safety solenoid valves, double solenoid valves, control valves
Brands: Dungs, Kromschröder, and other combustion control specialists.

Bearings & Linear Motion

Bearing solutions for rotating equipment, power transmission, and linear motion systems.

  • Deep groove, angular contact, self‑aligning ball bearings
  • Cylindrical, tapered, spherical, needle roller bearings
  • Thrust bearings, pillow block bearings, insert bearings
  • Linear bearings and guide rails
Brands: SKF, Timken, NTN, Nachi, NSK, INA, Koyo, THK, Thomson, Dodge, FAG, IKO, Asahi, Hiwin, and others.

Printing Industry Components

Components tailored for printing presses and related machinery.

  • Offset printing blankets
  • Compressible blankets
  • UV and conventional blankets
  • Sheet‑fed and web press blankets
Supports high‑quality, consistent print performance.

Engineering Vehicle Parts

Components for heavy equipment, construction machinery, and engineering vehicles.

  • Engine assemblies, cylinder blocks, heads, gaskets, oil pans, valve covers
  • Pistons, rings, connecting rods, crankshafts, bearings, flywheels, belts
  • Camshafts, valves, springs, rocker arms, lifters, timing systems
  • Fuel pumps, high‑pressure pumps, injectors, common rail systems, filters
Brands: CAT, Liugong, Shantui, Kalmar, Sany, Konecranes, XCMG, JCB, Toyota, Cummins, Volvo, ZF, Perkins, Hitachi, Komatsu, Weichai, Zoomlion, John Deere, Yanmar, SDLG, Bosch.

Filters, Fasteners, Cables & Auto Parts

Supporting components and consumables for industrial and automotive applications.

  • Filters: oil, fuel, air, hydraulic (Fleetguard, Mann, Donaldson, Parker, Hydac, Hifi Filter, John Deere, and others)
  • Fasteners: bolts, nuts, washers, screws, threaded rods, anchors, rivets
  • Cables: power, control, special, heat tracing, instrumentation, telecommunication, winding wires, building wires
  • Automotive parts for major global vehicle brands
Complements the core industrial and machinery parts portfolio.
